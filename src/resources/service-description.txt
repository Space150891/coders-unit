3)      Service Description
The iMatrix services from Sierra Telecom include tools for managing all aspects of a connected business.
iMatrix capabilities include:

*         iMatrix Connectivity solutions make it simple and quick to connect devices with unlimited scalability.

*         iMatrix Management features that simplify the tasks of provisioning, monitoring and updating devices and of authenticating and managing IoT Devices and users.

*         iMatrix Security features offer end-to-end protection of devices and the data they contain while in transit and idle.

*         iMatrix Analytics features allow transform of data and relationships into insightful information that can deliver greater value and better products.

*         iMatrix services also include professional services that can directly assist a company to transform itself into a connected business. This will offer a faster path to increased profits, optimized operations and better customer engagement.
Note: The standard iMatrix pricing model is Appendix II. The Apollo Application running on iMatrix has a 25% surcharge applicable at all levels.
